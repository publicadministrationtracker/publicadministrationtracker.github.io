How to Read This Project

Health shows the project’s balanced framework clearly: it includes some of the strongest positive claims in the dataset and some of the highest-scoring failures. Claims are grouped by policy area and impact direction. Many claims are still under review and will later include source links, confidence levels, cross-references, and notes about competing interpretations.
