How to read this page

Claims are grouped by topic and ranked using the project’s weighted scoring model. The score reflects impact, evidence strength, and policy directness.

Negative

These claims describe policy harms, risks, failures, legal conflicts, or institutional stress.

Positive

Benefits or strengthened capacity

These claims describe accomplishments, policy gains, added capacity, or improved outcomes.

Mixed

Tradeoffs or contested outcomes

These claims may involve real benefits alongside costs, limits, or unresolved interpretation.
