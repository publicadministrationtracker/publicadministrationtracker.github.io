How to Read This Project

Immigration contains legal, humanitarian, fiscal, and enforcement dimensions. Many positive or mixed claims describe capacity or enforcement expansion rather than broad consensus outcomes. Claims are grouped by policy area and impact direction. Many claims are still under review and will later include source links, confidence levels, cross-references, and notes about competing interpretations.
