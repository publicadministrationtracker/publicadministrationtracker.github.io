Public Administration Tracker

This project tracks claims, actions, and measurable outcomes of the current U.S. presidential administration.

The goal is to provide a transparent, data-driven public resource documenting policy decisions, impacts, and historical comparisons.

Project Stages

  • Stage 1 — Build master evidence tracker (150–250 claims)
  • Stage 2 — Publish structured, sourced database
  • Stage 3 — Present comparative analysis of pro and anti claims

Status

Site launched. Initial data collection in progress.

Independent research project. All claims will be documented with publicly available sources.
